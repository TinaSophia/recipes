Ingredients:
- 2 (10 oz) packages frozen chopped spinach
- 1 onion chopped
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 2 cloves garlic finely chopped
- 1 large glass of spicy marinara sauce (Arrabiata)
- 1 1/2 cups of water
- 2 cups cottage cheese
- 1 (8 oz) package shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
- chopped fresh parsley (1/2-1 cup)
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 egg
- 8 oz lasagna noodles
- tabelspoon of olive oil (extra virgin)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 deg. F
2. Over medium heat saute spinach in the olive oil, add onions. When the onions
are almost done add the garlic, basil, and oregano.
3. Add marinara sauce and water. Simmer for 15 minutes.
4. In a large bowl mix cottage cheese, mozzarella, parmesan, parsley, salt,
pepper, and the egg.
5. Prepare lasagna pan: start with spinach sauce, layer noodles, cheese mix
and so forth. Finish with sauce.
6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 45-55 minutes. Check with a fork to
test if noodles are soft before serving.
